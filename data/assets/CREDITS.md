## nb07_calcium_schematic.png

- **Title:** How calcium enters a neuron and drives the indicator's fluorescence
- **Author:** Original figure created for this course (SLEAP Social Behavior Lab).
- **Source:** Rendered programmatically with matplotlib (schematic only; no
  external image was used). Build script: `tools/build_calcium_schematic.py`.
- **License:** Original work by the course authors — no third-party image was
  incorporated. Free to use, modify, and redistribute within this repository.

This is an **original, hand-drawn (matplotlib) schematic**, not a fetched image.
No public-domain / CC image was downloaded, so no external attribution applies.
It depicts, in simplified form: extracellular Ca²⁺, membrane depolarization
opening a voltage-gated calcium channel, Ca²⁺ influx down its gradient, and the
GCaMP indicator binding Ca²⁺ and fluorescing (brightness tracking cell activity).
